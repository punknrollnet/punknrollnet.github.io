Description

This design shows "Kreuzberg" and a large number 36 in a college style font. Until 1993 Germany used a 4 digit post code. Berlin was 1000 and part of the district Kreuzberg was 36, a neighborhood known for its punk and alternative scenes. It is for people who have lived or stayed in Berlin Kreuzberg and hopefully have good memories worth being nostalgic about.
