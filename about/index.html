About

punknroll.net is a music video player for rock genres such as Garage Punk, Grunge, Psychobilly, Punk, Rock'n'Roll, Rockabilly. On the home page and the genre start pages the playlists are assembled from YouTube video submissions in relevant subreddits on reddit.

There is also a historical archive for each genre, where the top 100 submissions for each month based on reddit scores can be played continuously. The archive playlists were extracted from historical data collected and provided Jason Baumgartner.

punknroll.net was created by Ramiro Gómez to have a rock music player for discovering new music and listening to popular rock songs.
